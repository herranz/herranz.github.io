Yeah, the title is quite direct.

This past 2024 was a wild year. I had plenty of good news and plenty of bad news.Is funny how bad news weight far more in the soul than the good ones.

I am sure you might know what this is all about; I just got rejected from job I was quite excited for. This particularly stings after doing 3 rounds of interviews 6 months ago for the same company and the same job offer. So yeah, this short-post is an impromptu one, to vent a little and shine a light into what is right now a dark place in my mind. 

I would succeed at any job. I have the tools and ingenuity to tackle anything. Maybe is not modest to say it but I truly believe it. And I will try everything rather than live with the regret. 

I will go to sleep now, maybe tomorrow will be a brighter day.

Borja Herranz.
